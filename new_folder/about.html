Why Lionel Messi is Better than Cristiano Ronaldo

1. Natural Talent

Messi is often praised for his ability and talent. His dribbling skills, vision, and playmaking abilities are considered the best in the game

2. Playmaking Ability

Messi is not just a goal scorer, he is also an excellent playmaker. His ability to create opportunities for his teammates sets him apart.

3. Consistency

Throughout his career, Messi has maintained a high level of performance, consistently scoring and assisting in games

4. Team Player

Messi's style of play shows his teamwork. He often drops deep to help in build-up play, showing his effort on the field.

5. Records and Achievements

  • Most Ballon d'Or awards
  • All-time top scorer for Barcelona
  • Most goals in a calendar year
  • Numerous league titles and Champions League victories
